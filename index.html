Our Mission

Our Mission is to continue to be a thriving part of the Hamilton community. Allowing people from all walks of life to enjoy our sport.

Our History

Formed over 50 years ago in the old town of Hamilton. Self suffient from the start, it automatic became a sport focal point of the community. One of the priniple features was its ability to have its own premises which allowed the developement of top Scottish, British and European champions. These players did train every evening at little costs which was the enabler for there progression in the sport.

The fundementals have never changed in which all players are encouraged to develop there own coaching skills and pass on the Hamilton ethos, to the next generation. Hamilton were particular successfull during the 70's, 80's and 90's. With a brand of Judo which was synonomous with high energy, complex hip techniques and the ability to transion between the standing and ground work dispclines at speed, without a change quality.

The pillars of the clubs success are routed in the training belief that with uchi-comi and high intense ramdory sessions. Each individual player will develop into a rounded competitor due to the focused training.
